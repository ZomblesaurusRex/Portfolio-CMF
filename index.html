About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer, lover of design & graduate of UCSD Extension Web Development Boot Camp. I've always been told that my creativity was like a Kaleidoscope, so when it came to get to the code, i felt it only natural to pursue Kaleidocode Creations for myself!
Rather than defining myself by my past, I define myself by the work I strive to do & the energy with which I seek it out. Skills can be taught, but imagination is inherent. I strive to keep learning, continue challenging myself, and leave no stone unturned.
Lets create a beautiful, new adventure together!
